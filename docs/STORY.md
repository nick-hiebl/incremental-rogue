# The Story

The player is organizing some kind of conspiracy. With different choices it will be a different kind of conspiracy.

The game starts with a standardised Act I, and from there branches into three major parts.

## Major branches

- Anti-science
- Occult power
- New religion

## Sub-branches / ideas

### Anti-science

- Anti-vaxx
- Anti-GMO
- UFO investigator
- QAnon?

### Occult power

- Blood cultists
- Demon worship
- Archdevils
- Mark of the X (major blessing)

### New religion

- Prophets
- Pontifexes
- Blessing of X (major blessing)
